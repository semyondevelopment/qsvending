Our Story

Built in Brisbane. Run by People Who Pick Up the Phone.

We started QS Vending for one reason: vending in Queensland was broken. Businesses were being charged install fees, locked into 2-year contracts, and waiting weeks for a restock that should have taken hours.

The national players treat Queensland like a line item. We treat it like home — because it is home. Our install crew, account managers and stock team are all Brisbane-based.

The model is simple: you pay nothing, we earn from product sales, the machine stays full, and the relationship continues because it's working — not because of a contract you signed three years ago and forgot about.
